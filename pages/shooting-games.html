Shooting Games

Play as a number of marksmen in a wide range of settings, firing at any adversaries that get in your way as you sneak through the levels. Make lessons with a variety of firearms.     How to play Shooting Games?

    All our shooting can be controlled with keyboard and mouse. First look into the description of the games, often you can find the controls there. Else these are the most commonly used controls for the PC:
    • WASD - Move Around
    • Mouse - Aim
    • Left Mouse Click - Shoot
    • Right Mouse Click - Zoom In
    • Spacebar - Jump
    • Shift - Sprint
    • R - Reload
